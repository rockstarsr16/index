Lose your knack
for losing things.

Ping it. Find it.

Misplacing something like your wallet doesn’t have
to be a big deal when it has AirTag attached.
You can play a sound on the built-in speaker by going to
the new Items tab in the Find My app,
or say “Hey Siri, find my wallet”.
If it’s hiding nearby -
like under the couch or in the next room —
just follow the sound and your search is over.
